The Lion King is an animated film set in the African savannah, following the journey of a young lion cub named Simba.
After his father, Mufasa, is tragically killed by his uncle Scar, Simba is forced into exile.
Scar takes over the Pride Lands, leading it into ruin.
As Simba grows up away from home, he learns about responsibility, courage, and the importance of his rightful place as king.
With the help of his friends Timon and Pumbaa, Simba returns to challenge Scar, reclaim his kingdom, and restore peace to the Pride Lands.
